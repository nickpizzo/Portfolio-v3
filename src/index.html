My name is Nick Pizzo and I am a Front End Developer focusing on user-facing design and development. I strive to bring the mind of both a programmer and an artist to my work. Having previous professional experience in other technical fields, and also a long history of involvement in performance art, the perfect marriage of logic and art has always been a familiar place.

I’ve lived a lifetime with a passion for decentralized technology and the wonders it does for the progression of humanity. With the internet the world is opened, the barriers fall, the separation ends, the togetherness starts, the collaboration explodes, and the helping emerges. These are the communities I want to build, improve, and be a part of.

I’m currently based in Las Vegas, and I'm working on some exciting things in several promising young industries. Before that I was part of cohort number four at The Iron Yard Tampa Bay. I’m always on the lookout for more professional connections, and I’m especially interested in speaking, educational, and collaboration opportunities. Thanks for visiting my site! If you would like to talk more let’s get in touch.
